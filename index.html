Movie Ranking Project

1.Pulp Fiction

This movie is a Quentin Tarantino masterpiece which displays violence and gore such that the story is divided into subplots of the movie
and are connected or intertwined again in a natural manner which makes it watchable many times and still expect a thrill.

2.Breaking bad

An amazing thrilling series which is made to keep the viewers interested and indulged throughout the entire series.
Story depicts the life and decision making of a proficient chemist who has been diagnosed with cancer but has to make
enough money to provide for his family but pursues a rather criminal way which makes up for many of the twists in the series.

3.The Departed

Although I can't remember the specifics of the movie, I still remember being engrossed with the storyline and also
remarkable cast selection which makes it an unforgettable movie. The police department is adamant to pursue a criminal mob boss
but they have an agent go undercover to be a part of his gang, but at the same time the criminal boss has a mole in the police department
and these 2 individuals try and expose one another.
